About Civil At Hand

Engineering excellence since 2016 — trusted across India for precision, integrity, and transformative design.

Our Story

Built on Expertise,
Driven by Passion

Civil At Hand was founded with a single mission: to make professional civil engineering services accessible to every Indian family and business — regardless of their location or budget. What started as a small consultancy in Punjab has grown into a pan-India firm trusted by 150+ clients.

We believe that your home deserves expert-level attention. Every floor plan we draw, every structural design we deliver, and every BOQ we prepare carries our commitment to accuracy, safety, and beauty.

From the first plot survey to the final blueprint handover, we are your engineering partner — not just a service provider.
