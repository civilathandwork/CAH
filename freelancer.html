Open Roles

Skills & Freelance Roles
We're Actively Hiring

Whether you're a fresh graduate with the right skills or an experienced professional, there's a place for you here.

📐

AutoCAD / CAD Designer

Proficient in AutoCAD 2D drafting — floor plans, elevations, sections, site plans. Experience with architectural or civil drawings preferred. Strong accuracy required.

Tools: AutoCAD, DraftSight
🏗️

Structural Engineer

Civil engineers with RCC design knowledge — beam, column, slab, and foundation design. IS code compliance essential. Software or manual calculations accepted.

Tools: STAAD Pro, ETABS, or manual
🖥️

3D Visualization Designer

Create stunning 3D exterior renders and walkthroughs of residential and commercial buildings. A strong visual portfolio is required to apply for this role.

Tools: SketchUp, 3ds Max, Lumion, V-Ray
📊

BOQ / Estimator

Skilled in preparing detailed Bill of Quantities, item-wise rate analysis, and construction cost estimation. Proficiency in Excel is mandatory.

Tools: MS Excel, CostX
🏘️

House Plan Designer

Experience in residential floor plan design — space planning, vastu compliance, and client customisation. Must be able to handle revision requests efficiently.

Tools: AutoCAD, Revit, or equivalent
📋

Site Supervisor / PMC

Experienced in field supervision, quality control, and contractor coordination on residential or commercial projects. Field presence required in Punjab/Haryana.

Location: Punjab / Haryana preferred
