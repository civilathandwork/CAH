Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Nikhil —
Your Site Engineering Mentor

I still remember my first day at a construction site. I had a B.Tech degree but had zero clue what to do when the foreman asked me to check the column reinforcement.

No textbook prepared me for the real site. That frustration — and the months I spent learning the hard way — is exactly why I built Civil At Hand.

My mission: give every civil engineering student and fresher the practical site knowledge college never teaches. For free, in plain language, with real examples.

"The gap between a civil engineering degree and real site work is huge. I'm here to bridge it."
— Nikhil, Founder, Civil At Hand
