2. Description of Services

Civil At Hand provides the following professional civil engineering services:

  • House Planning (2D/3D): Floor plan design, layout preparation, 3D visualisation
  • Structural Design: RCC design, beam/column/slab calculations per IS codes
  • BOQ & Cost Estimation: Bill of quantities, material take-off, project cost estimates
  • PDF to AutoCAD Conversion: Conversion of scanned/PDF drawings to editable CAD files
  • Technical Consultancy: Expert advice on construction methods, materials, and compliance
  • Student Guidance: Academic project support, thesis assistance, internship guidance

All services are delivered digitally. We reserve the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any service with reasonable notice.
