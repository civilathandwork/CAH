How to Use

How to Use These Civil
Engineering Calculators

📊

Construction Cost Calculator

Enter your total built-up area in sq.ft, select your construction quality (basic/standard/premium/luxury), and number of floors. The tool gives you a realistic cost range in Indian Rupees based on current market rates.

🧱

Material Estimator (BOQ)

Enter floor area and number of floors to get approximate quantities for cement (bags), sand (cft), aggregate (cft), steel (kg), and bricks. Great for initial material planning and contractor discussions.

📐

Area Calculator

Select your plot shape (rectangle, circle, triangle, or trapezoid), enter dimensions in feet, and get the area in sq.ft, sq.m, and sq.yd simultaneously. Ideal for plot area verification before buying land.

🪣

Concrete Mix Calculator

Enter the concrete volume you need in cubic metres (m³), select the grade (M15, M20, or M25), and get the required quantities of cement bags, sand, and aggregate. Based on IS 10262 guidelines.
